Welcome to Ketchup Talks!

This series serves like a condiment to the mouthwatering snack that is research. Every week (or two weeks), doctoral students at IIIT-Delhi get together and deliberate on a host of topics dealing with research ranging from published work, works in progress, or even simply interesting ideas.

We are looking for speakers! If you are doctoral student at IIIT-D, and are interested in enlightening your fellow researchers, please use this google form to nominate yourself as a speaker.
