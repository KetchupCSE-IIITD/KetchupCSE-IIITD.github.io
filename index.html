Welcome to Ketchup Talks!

This talk series serves as a condiment to the mouthwatering snack, which is research. Every week (or two weeks), doctoral students at IIIT-Delhi get together and deliberate on various topics dealing with research ranging from published work, works in progress, or even simply interesting ideas.

We are looking for speakers! If you are a doctoral student at IIIT-D and are interested in enlightening your fellow researchers, please use this google form to nominate yourself as a speaker.
